II. Traditional Medications: Pros and Cons

Conventional allergy medications, such as antihistamines and decongestants, provide quick relief from symptoms. This section delves into the pros and cons of traditional medications, highlighting their effectiveness in symptom management while addressing potential drawbacks such as side effects and dependency.

III. Immunotherapy: A Game-Changer in Allergy Treatment

Immunotherapy, including allergy shots (subcutaneous immunotherapy) and sublingual immunotherapy (SLIT), represents a groundbreaking approach to allergy treatment. This section explores how immunotherapy works, its long-term benefits, and its role in desensitizing the immune system to allergens.
